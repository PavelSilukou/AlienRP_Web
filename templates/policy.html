Privacy policy The AlienRP website The website does NOT store your personal information. There are NO registration forms, Google Analytics, social buttons, cookies on the website. The AlienRP application The application stores your personal information such as your email address and password on your computer in encrypted form. The AlienRP application does NOT send any personal information to the AlienRP server.
