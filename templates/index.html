About AlienRP is a completely free, NONOFFICIAL desktop application for music playback on the following stations:
  • DI.FM (Digitally Imported)
  • ROCKRADIO.COM
  • RadioTunes
  • ClassicalRadio.com
  • JAZZRADIO.com
Features Playback of any channel on the following stations:
  • DI.FM (Digitally Imported)
  • ROCKRADIO.COM
  • RadioTunes
  • ClassicalRadio.com
  • JAZZRADIO.com
  • Adding to favorite channels and removing from favorite channels
  • Voting for favorite tracks
  • Global hotkey support to control the player
  • Windows 7 / 8 / 10 support (64-bit and 32-bit versions are available)
